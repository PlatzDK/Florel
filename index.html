Welcome

Florel is a cosy forest cabin offering direct access to a lake and a nearby stream — perfect for families and fishing enthusiasts. Explore local spots, rent gear or book a guide.

Fishing activities

Denmark offers varied angling: coastal and sea fishing, river and lake fishing, and put & take ponds.

  • Coastal fishing for sea trout, garfish and mackerel (spring / late summer / autumn).
  • Lake fishing: pike, perch, zander, trout and eel.
  • River fishing in clear streams — local day permits often required.
  • Put & take: family-friendly and usually open daily.
  • Boat, kayak or belly-boat angling where permitted.

Practical info

Boat & kayak rentals, tackle shops and local guides are available nearby. Contact us for help with day tickets and guide bookings.
