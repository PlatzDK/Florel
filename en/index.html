About the house

The cosy wooden cabin includes a living room with wood stove, a fully equipped kitchen and two bedrooms sleeping four guests. The bathroom has hot water and the large terrace overlooks the lake and forest.

Fishing activities

Denmark offers varied angling: coastal and sea fishing, river and lake fishing, and put & take ponds.

  • Coastal fishing for sea trout, garfish and mackerel (spring / late summer / autumn).
  • Lake fishing: pike, perch, zander, trout and eel.
  • River fishing in clear streams — local day permits often required.
  • Put & take ponds: family-friendly and usually open daily.
  • Boat, kayak or belly-boat angling where permitted.

Local fishing spots

  • Bølling Lake (≈2 km): free shore fishing; pike, perch; shelters and canoe/rowboat access.
  • Skyggehale Put & Take (≈5 km): family-friendly; toilets and cleaning tables; regularly stocked trout.
  • Silkeborg Lakes / Langsø (Gudenå) (≈15 km): large lakes; pike, perch, zander, trout (license + day-ticket).
  • Tollundgaard Put & Take (Pårup) (≈10 km): classic put & take pond.
  • Karup River (≈30 km): river angling famed for sea trout — local permits required.
  • Hjarbæk Fjord (Limfjord) (≈55 km): fjord/coastal fishing for sea trout and garfish.
